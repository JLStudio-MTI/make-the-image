About Make the Image

Make the Image is a collaborative pixel-art canvas inspired by r/Place. Everyone shares one big board and can place one pixel every few minutes. Watch the picture form over time with the community.

How it works

  • Pick a color from the palette.
  • Tap a cell on the canvas to place a pixel.
  • There’s a cooldown between placements to keep it fair.
  • Choose a permanent username (no login). Your name appears on hover.
  • The leaderboard shows who has placed the most pixels.

Rules

  • Keep it friendly—no hate or illegal content.
  • No attempts to bypass the cooldown.
  • Usernames must follow the guidelines.
